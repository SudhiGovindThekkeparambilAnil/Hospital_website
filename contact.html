Frequently Asked Questions

Our medical center is open Monday through Sunday from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM.

While walk-in appointments are welcome, we highly recommend scheduling an appointment in advance to minimize wait times and ensure that you receive timely care. You can schedule appointments by calling our reception desk or using our online booking system.

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please check with your insurance provider or contact our billing department for specific information about coverage and payment options.

We offer a wide range of medical services including primary care, preventive care, vaccinations, chronic disease management, minor surgical procedures, laboratory testing, and more. Our experienced team of healthcare professionals is dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate care to our patients.

The safety and well-being of our patients and staff are our top priorities. We have implemented rigorous cleaning and disinfection protocols, limited the number of patients in our waiting areas, implemented social distancing measures, require face masks for all individuals inside the facility, and offer telemedicine appointments for certain types of visits. Please don't hesitate to contact us if you have any questions or concerns about our COVID-19 safety measures.
